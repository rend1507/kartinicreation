Why us?

  • Handmade with Love: Every piece is crafted by hand with attention to detail, ensuring each creation is one-of-a-kind and made with care by our founder, Putri Kapandeyan.
  • Fully Customized: At Kartini Creations, you’re the designer! From the size and color to the yarn and pattern, we offer full customization to match your unique style and preferences.
  • Quality Materials: We use only high-quality yarns and materials, so your knitwear and crochet items are not only beautiful but durable and long-lasting.
  • Support Local Craftsmanship: When you choose Kartini Creations, you’re supporting homegrown talent and traditional craftsmanship, helping to keep Indonesian arts alive.
  • Personal Connection: Our small, home-based business allows us to focus on building relationships with each customer, offering a personalized shopping experience you won’t find elsewhere.
  • Eco-Friendly Approach: By creating handmade products on demand, we reduce waste and promote sustainable fashion practices.

ABOUT US

At Kartini Creations, we bring the beauty of handmade craftsmanship to life through our unique, customized knitwear and crochet arts. Each piece is lovingly crafted by hand by our founder, Putri Kapandeyan, ensuring that every item is not just a product but a personal creation made with care.

Whether you're looking for the perfect cozy sweater, a unique crochet accessory, or a bespoke piece tailored to your style, we offer a range of customization options. Choose your preferred size, color, yarn, and pattern, and we'll create something special just for you.

Based in Malang City, Indonesia, we take pride in supporting traditional crafts while offering modern designs that fit your personal aesthetic. Kartini Creations is your home for personalized, handmade wearables that bring warmth and individuality to every wardrobe.
